Our Roadmap

Follow our journey as we build the Krew Coin universe. Key milestones and future developments are outlined below, based on our whitepaper v1.1.

Q2 2025

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Develop $KREW, NFT minting/staking, krew formation, treasury mechanics.
  • Finalize 5 NFT krew designs.
  • Release White Paper V1.1, launch website, Discord, Twitter.
Q3 2025

Phase 2: Pre-Launch

  • Complete smart contracts for coin minting, $pKrew, liquidity locking.
  • Audit contracts, test on Solana Testnet with 1,000 beta testers.
  • Develop Krew Dashboard UI.
  • Expand marketing, explore launchpad partnerships.
Q4 2025

Phase 3: Launch

  • Deploy to Solana Mainnet, $KREW TGE, initial liquidity on Raydium.
  • Conduct NFT sale, enable staking.
  • Activate treasury funding, $pKrew earning, coin minting, LP creation.
  • Boost community growth and support.
Q1 2026

Phase 4: Expansion

  • Enhance dashboard, voting, NFT options.
  • Monitor minted coins, expand DEX integrations.
  • Strengthen marketing and partnerships.
Q2 2026+

Phase 5: Scaling

  • Develop mobile interface, explore Solana scaling.
  • Research cross-chain bridges.
  • Transition to DAO governance, add krew competitions, expand NFT utility.
